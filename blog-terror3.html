Terror in the Dark
Part 3 is Here

By Graham Maclin  ·  March 21, 2026

It's finally here. Terror in the Dark Part 3 is the book I've been most excited — and most nervous — to write. Bob and Josh are back, it's Halloween, and things are about to get a lot worse than a bunch of raccoons in a backyard.

This time, the stakes are personal. EP, the mysterious biker from Bonbon Street, has a raccoon tattoo and knows something he's not telling. The raccoons have uranium guns. And Hangman Lane — the street Josh has never been able to talk about — turns out to be the most dangerous place in Miami.

Going deeper

One thing I really wanted to do in Part 3 was go deeper into who Bob and Josh actually are. In the first book, they were just two kids at a sleepover. Now they have a secret underground passageway, a hidden pool, an elevator, and a soda bar. They've built something incredible together — which makes it all the more terrifying when it starts to get destroyed.

The raccoons blowing through a titanium wall with liquid oxygen felt like the right way to show that the enemy has leveled up too. These aren't the same raccoons from Book 1. Something has changed.

"A raccoon with sunglasses and a boombox walked out of the bushes. It was blasting the Mission Impossible theme."

That scene

Yes, there is a raccoon with sunglasses and a boombox. Yes, it is blasting the Mission Impossible theme. Yes, the raccoons drop from the sky like little Tom Cruises. I wrote that scene and laughed for about five minutes straight. But then EP walks through the crowd, and it stops being funny very fast.

Josh's mom

This is the part of the book I'm most proud of. We find out that Josh's mom was taken by the raccoons on Hangman Lane when Josh was only ten months old. Josh has never talked about it. Bob found out from Josh's dad, secretly, behind Josh's back. That detail changes everything about why Josh acts the way he does — and why Hangman Lane matters so much.

I hope you enjoy Part 3. You can read Chapter Three for free on this site, and the full book is available now. Check your locks. You've been warned.
