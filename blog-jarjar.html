Behind the Story:
Jar Jar's Revenge

By Graham Maclin  ·  March 14, 2026

Every story starts with a question. For Jar Jar's Revenge, the question was simple: what if Jar Jar Binks wasn't just a bumbling sidekick? What if he had been quietly planning something for a very, very long time?

That question stuck with me. Once I started pulling on that thread, the whole story unraveled in the best possible way — two timelines, a galaxy full of secrets, and a character who is much more dangerous than anyone gave him credit for.

Two timelines

One of the most fun challenges of writing this book was jumping between 1653 and 1694. The earlier timeline shows how Jar Jar first crossed paths with Darth Man on the island of Botox — and how that meeting changed everything. The later timeline is where the revenge actually happens, on one dark and windy night that sets the whole galaxy on edge.

Keeping track of what Jar Jar knew in each timeline, and what he didn't know yet, was like a puzzle. But that's the part I enjoyed most.

"What if Jar Jar Binks had been planning something for a very, very long time?"

Darth Man

Darth Man was the character that surprised me most. He started as a mysterious figure in the shadows and became one of my favorite characters to write. There's something really interesting about a villain who has his own code — who shows up forty-one years later like no time has passed at all.

And yes, Darth Tom Hanks is in there too. Some things just have to be in a story.

What's next

I'm not ready to say much yet — but Jar Jar's story isn't finished. The purple strand stretched across the sky means something. And I intend to find out what.

Thanks for reading. Stay stealthy out there.
