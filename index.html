A Word
About Graham

"Every story begins with a question I can't stop asking myself."

Graham Maclin is a well-known author of Terror in the Dark and Jar Jar's Revenge. His work spans science fiction, thriller, and fantasy — united by fast-moving plots, bold heroes, and a sense of adventure that never lets up.

When he's not writing, Graham loves playing football, bouncing on his trampoline, and diving into a good book. He lives in Virginia with his family.
